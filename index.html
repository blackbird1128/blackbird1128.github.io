Welcome to my blog

Here you will find read-only articles about my projects, various ideas and some ramblings. Opinion expressed here are purely mine and not related to my past, present or future employers.

If you enjoy the content made here, you can simply share it with your friends or colleagues using various social media. But this place will probably never have a way to discuss the content directly as I don't wanna deal with bots and moderation.

Who am I ?

As I enjoy my privacy, I will not disclose my real name here. You can call me Jack, or Alice. I'm a 21 year old student in computer science, currently living in France. I started programming in high school with a friend. At first I was using C++ (yeah not the brightest idea) and Python and I learned a lot of concepts, some too quickly to understand them correctly. But it made me realize something, I loved programming, and for my high school self, it was one of the coolest thing ever. So computer science was the obvious choice for college. A few years later, i still love programming and I decided to write this blog to share my passion.

What is this blog about ?

Well, it's about a lot of things, i plan to write mostly about my computer science projects but also about books I enjoyed, movie I watched and loved, and maybe some random thoughts about life.

This blog is also a way to write in english, as I'm not a native speaker, it offers me a way to improve my english.

Who is this blog for ?

This blog is meant to be accessible to everyone, technical or not with any background. I will try to make this blog as accessible as possible to everyone, with a simple design and a clear writing style.
